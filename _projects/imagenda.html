By Patrick Harris, Alex Li, Vanshil Shah, Rustem Khamitov, and Atharva Gokhale


About

Imagenda is a mobile application to scan a real-life schedule and digitize it. Using the app, the user takes a photo of a schedule on paper or uploads a screenshot from a webpage. Events from the image are instantly recognized, processed, and imported into their preferred calendar application (e.g. Google Calendar or iCal). In bustling conferences or meetings, schedules are often distributed in the form of brochures or websites that have arbitrary formats. This application allows users to import any third-party schedule to their personal e-calendar with ease.
